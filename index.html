The chart shows the simulated DPS of different secondary distributions of the otherwise same profile. If the profile has for example 1000 secondary stats, a dot (profile) with a Relative value of 30% in crit uses 300 of that rating as its crit rating to run a simulation. The resulting DPS is shown as the color. Red is the best result, blue the worst. Most of the time best and worst are pretty close.
