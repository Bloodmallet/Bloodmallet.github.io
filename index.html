Stacked trinket charts

This site provides an overview for all available trinkets for all specs in World of Warcraft. Please use the trinket analysis of your spec's theorycrafters for more accuracy.

To generate the data, I'm using SimulationCraft and its standard profiles. All limitations applying to SimulationCraft apply to these graphs as well. Your own equipment, legendaries and talents change the order of trinkets for your character. Therefore, simming different trinkets against each other is the most effective way to know which performs best. The easiest way to do so is by heading to RAIDBOTS, which uses SimulationCraft for you. Try it out.

Used settings

  • fight_style: patchwerk and beastlord
  • fight_length: 300sec
  • target_error: 0.1% (patchwerk) and 0.25% (beastlord)
  • ptr: false
  • base profiles: T20M (no special profiles like DfA or gambling were used)
  • a versatility stat stick at ilevel 880 was used in conjunction with the ones in the graph

I might add dps-trinket charts for tank specs in the near future. Because tank specs are supported quite differently, beware of strange results. As long as there are no standard tank profiles (T20M), I won't be able to create these charts.

Disclaimer: Beastlord simulations suffer from multiple weaknesses and these graphs should be taken with a huge boulder of salt. The standard profiles are most likely optimized for single target; itemwise (secondaries) and talentwise. As I don't change either it's very much possible that the results you see here, don't reflect the potential for your spec. In addition it's possible that the standard APL does not consider AoE fights. Make sure to check out your theorycrafters resources!
