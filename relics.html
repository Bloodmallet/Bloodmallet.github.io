Crucible Charts

This site provides an overview of all light, shadow crucible traits, and damage traits for all specs in World of Warcraft using SimulationCraft with Github - Bloodyrelics. Please use the trait analysis of your spec's theorycrafters for a more in depth view on these traits.

Looking for help: With Battle for Azeroth looming on the horizon, it's time to improve; and YOU can help to make this resource better. There are multiple ways to do so. Designing, coding, requesting data, pointing out flaws, to name a few. All you need is a GitHub account and you can start adding comments and issues here .

Disclaimer: Beastlord simulations suffer from multiple weaknesses and these graphs should be taken with a huge boulder of salt. The standard profiles are most likely optimized for single target; itemwise (secondaries) and talentwise. As I don't change either it's very much possible that the results you see here, don't reflect the potential for your spec. In addition it's possible that the standard APL does not consider AoE fights. Make sure to check out your theorycrafters resources!

Please be aware that some Light-Traits have a shared RPPM (proc rate) of their damage and healing effects. When simulating these traits the actors aren't hit unless they are tanks. Which results in more damage procs than you can expect for a real fight.
