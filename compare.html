Trinket Comparison

The Trinket Compare Tool provides a quick way of comparing two trinkets for any DPS spec in the game. To use the tool, simply select your class, spec, and the two trinkets you wish to compare. If you have any questions, feel free to ask slanderman#8657 on the Earthshrine Discord.
